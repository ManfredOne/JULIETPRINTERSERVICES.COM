JULIET PRINTER SERVICES

This company deals in computer services and we buy and sell new and used computers. We sell new printers, phones and repairing them as well. We are located 25 miles along Kampala - Gulu highway in Bombo town

When you buy a new computer, we give you a warrant of five months and in case of any problem, you can swap or change to another machine of your choice for free.

Our customers are treated as the first priority and we do business with anyone interested in working with us.
